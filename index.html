about me.

I am a Web Developer with problem solving skills who loves to create. I seek out challenges and have an innate ability to adapt to any situation. My history as a hands-on tradesman, has given me the ability to compare old world and new world methods, which means I can offer many perspectives to tackling almost any task. My self-motivated and loyal nature means that I can keep moving forward despite the hardships of a project no matter how daunting.

Once I start on a project, I obsess until it is done, down to the last snippet. I love the constant learning and mastering of code and everything those results can bring.

While I do love a good frontend framework, exploring and mastering backend is a high priority. All in all, I currently have experience building with React, Angular, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, MongoDB (including full CRUD and/or RESTful MEAN stack apps). Vue, Sass, and Python are on my horizon as well.

Gifted with a knack for front-end aesthetic, I can build a clean and modern presentation for just about any need. I love using CSS frameworks like Bulma, Materialize, and Bootstrap. As for supersets of CSS, Sass/SCSS are on my horizon as well.

My skills in Javascript have earned me the ability to build in both jQuery, React, and Angular. Vue is also of interest to me despite its lack of corporate representation. But practicing and utilzing vanilla Javascript will always be a high priority.

With experience using Ruby on Rails, MongoDB and SQL, I'm proud to call myself a full stack Web developer. Whether it be a solo project or a massive undertaking, no matter how big the task I'm always up to building a solid backend/API.
